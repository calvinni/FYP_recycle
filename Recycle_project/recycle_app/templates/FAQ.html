FAQs


What does our application do?

ReCircle is a web application for the residents of Singapore to redeem different types of vouchers for recycling and apply for recyclable pick up services by our Recircle volunteers.


How can I redeem the vouchers?

You get points by recycling at affiliated recycle bins or book an appointment for recyclable pick-up after logging in with a registered account. After the recyclable pick-up has been confirmed on our side, the points will be distributed accordingly. This process usually take 2-4 working days.
